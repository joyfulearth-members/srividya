# Day 10 – Kamalā: The Abundant Lotus

🔹 Sanskrit Verse

```sanskritized-english
SriLakshmeemsubham nriputangeem
Kamalajyotimaye vichintayami
Deivagunasheela ujwalaswethaurja
MaaKamalamalapraveshey bhagyanti
Lalatamahabindikumkumat
Netraruchi lavinyadharasurachitam
Chitvilasashakti srivarahiswarupitam
Ekadasi Gajavahinyat SriKamalamatajalodhari
Ahirmahiryadava twamsevaka
Vigyanakalakavyabalam siddhivishesharupini swarnotbhavamchandrasheelam
Supindriyajanmobhavat
Tavakamalarupacharanadhuli
Siddhartam
Oṁ Śrīṁ Hrīṁ Klīṁ Aiṁ Kamalavāsinyai Svāhā.
```

---

🔸 Poetic Translation

```poetic-translation
O Lotus-Radiance, bliss of sovereign wealth,
You who wear beauty like breath,
Your aura is gold and moon-kissed,
Your path—soft petals of awakening.
You ride the elephant of grace,
Bearing insight, poetry, and sacred power.
I bathe in your dust, O Goddess of fulfillment—
Let destiny bloom where your feet fall.
```

---

✨ Inner Meaning

```inner-meaning
Kamalā is the completion—the blossomed crown chakra. She brings spiritual and material
siddhi, the ripened fruition of grace. She is Lakṣmī unbound, the inner wealth that reflects outer
harmony. Her presence is contentment and majesty aligned.
```
