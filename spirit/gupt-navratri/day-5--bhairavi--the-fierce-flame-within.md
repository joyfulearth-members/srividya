# Day 5 – Bhairavī: The Fierce Flame Within

🔹 Sanskrit Verse

```sanskritized-english
MaaBhairavi mahachamundeshwari
Kritvilochana samudradayini
Matrubhavani jagatjanani
vaksiddhastuta chittirupini
Vastunichheda bhayankari
Ugrakrodha balikakriyakridaapi
Vinashavinyasa pavitrom maatam
Vishwasasneha atmanirmayee
Twamakalabhairavi kalacharaupamohiteem
Bhairavabhakira shubhanghnada bharani
Asatyomrityu karana mahamayeem
Aumaimkleem sow. Mahabhairavyayswaha
```

---

🔸 Poetic Translation

```poetic-translation
O Bhairavī, Great Mother of the fierce play,
You whose third eye births oceans of fire,
Your roar melts falsehood into purity.

All form returns to source in your touch.
You are the love beyond reason,
The shadow-destroyer, time-eater.
Worshipped in silence and storm,
I surrender to your undivided flame.
```

---

✨ Inner Meaning

```inner-meaning
She is the force that unmakes what no longer serves. Bhairavī destroys inner weakness and
false structures. She is the agni of transformation, especially potent during rites of passage. Her
worship brings courage, sharp clarity, and the end of dependence.
```
