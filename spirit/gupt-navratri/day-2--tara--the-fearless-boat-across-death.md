# Day 2 – Tārā: The Fearless Boat Across Death

🔹 Sanskrit Verse

```sanskritized-english
MaaTARA bhayeem
Namakaree Kasipuradheshwari,
Bhikshamdehikripakaram mana hareem
Mataa smashana pushpanjali
Lokeyka janmanakshatreem
Jatadharakalpavimochineem
Bhairavaputri sadamaheem
Drishtipushthi nivarineem
Bhadrarupa kavachasodhaneem
Sudheykaveenasmriteem
Kasidevanirochaneem
Hrim tarayai namah deiveem
Bhayabhakshu kalanideem
Vishamitramohinee
Subaadandamrita architey
Jyotirmartreem Maamuma mokshadayini
```

---

🔸 Poetic Translation

```poetic-translation
O Tārā, Queen of Kāśī, dissolver of fear—
You who wear skulls and grant radiant peace,
Accept this flower-offering from the cremation ground of my mind.
You, time’s liberator and the moon’s secret rhythm,

Dispel illusions and nourish sight.
Bhairava’s daughter, ever-mahī, protect my voice.
You cleanse the armor of fate with your bhadra-rūpa.
O Devi of wisdom and liberation—
May you lead this soul to the Moksha-light.
```
