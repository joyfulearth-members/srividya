# Day 6 – Chhinnamastā: The Self-Offering Flash

🔹 Sanskrit Verse

```sanskritized-english
Chintamanikarishyami
jyotirbhayabhavanam
Ahankaranacivyamamah
Divyadevichinnamastey
ShrishaBhaktiSindhu sadhyaNadeem
Parikshetru hitamohanangeem
Kritaabhimana khanditadevi
Marmarakshana kshamadhatu avirguneem
Sodasivarsheykanyam rakthajivanabhojanampurayatee
Sankalpey chitshakti punarjanmaniatmam
Tamaskaley upadarshiti
ॐ ींंऐंववडारि यैछि नमतायैनमः
```

---

🔸 Poetic Translation

```poetic-translation
I call you, lightning-blooded goddess of truth.
Chhinnamastā, beheaded yet unshaken—
You nourish life from your own severed crown,
Breaking ego with a smile of fire.
You guard the marrow of resolve,
Revealing light within darkness.
With thunderbolts of knowing,
You awaken power in sacrifice.
```

---

✨ Inner Meaning

```inner-meaning
She is the force that unmakes what no longer serves. Bhairavī destroys inner weakness and
false structures. She is the agni of transformation, especially potent during rites of passage. Her
worship brings courage, sharp clarity, and the end of dependence.
```
